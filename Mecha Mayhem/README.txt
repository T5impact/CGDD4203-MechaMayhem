Mecha Mayhem

Our game is an endless runner with boss fights. 
The game consists of two boss fights and an endless mode at the very end. 
The boss fights have their own unique attacks and attack patterns.
You fight the bosses by firing missiles which you pick up as you run.

Controls:
Move Left and Right - Swipe left and right 
Jump - Swipe up
Hover - tap and hold finger on screen while jumping
Pick up missile, fuels, and orbs by running into them
Shoot missile - Tap the missile button
(For AR, aim the missile by pointing the camera at where you want to shoot)
